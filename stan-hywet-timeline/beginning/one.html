Once upon a time, a boy was born in Norton, Ohio.

“He's so adorable!” his mom, Catherine, beamed when he was first born.

“Let's name him Frank,” his dad, John, declared.

“Frank Seiberling, you'll be an important addition to the family,” he added, talking to his weeping baby.

Little did either of his parents know, Frank would become the most important member of his family in the next few decades; he would become a pioneer in the rubber industry.
